# Design System: Ladang Lima — Agriculture-Luxe

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
A "Balanced Minimalism" interface. It is premium and elegant, but restrained—neither overwhelming in scale nor cramped in density (Density 5). Confident symmetry with subtle asymmetric accents. The atmosphere is sophisticated and approachable, prioritizing readability and functional elegance over dramatic scale.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Earth Canvas** (#FDFCFB) — Primary background, cleaner warm white
- **Pure Surface** (#FFFFFF) — Floating containers
- **Forest Ink** (#052e16) — Primary text
- **Muted Silt** (#64748b) — Secondary text, descriptions
- **Whisper Root** (rgba(5,46,22,0.08)) — 1px structural lines
- **Sunlit Amber** (#B45309) — Refined accent, less saturated for elegance
- **Banned:** Pure black (#000000), neon colors, heavy gradients.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Display:** Instrument Serif — Controlled scale (max 5rem on desktop), weight-driven hierarchy.
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans — 16px/1.6 leading, max 65ch width for perfect legibility.
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono — 12px for metadata and data points.
- **Hierarchy:** No extreme jumps in font size. Smooth, mathematical scaling.

## 4. Component Stylings
- **Buttons:** Subtle rounded corners (0.75rem), not full-round. Tactile feedback.
- **Cards:** Refined rounding (1.5rem). Very soft, wide shadows (Apple-style).
- **Layout:** Standardized section padding (py-24 to py-32).
- **Icons:** Thin stroke (1.5px), consistent 24px size.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Grid:** Balanced 2 and 3 column grids.
- **Hero:** Centered or Split, but with human-scale typography.
- **Whitespace:** Generous but intentional. Not "wasted" space.
- **Mobile:** Clean stack with 2rem horizontal padding.
